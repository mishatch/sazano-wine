Hotel Complex

Nestled along the serene banks of the Dzusa River, "Sazano Wine" Hotel Complex was established in 2017. Originally comprising of two charming buildings and 170 years-old antique wooden house, the complex underwent a remarkable expansion in 2023. Today, it boasts a collection of 31 distinctively designed rooms, providing accommodation for up to 78 guests. Inclusivity is at the heart of our offerings, with a specially adapted room and comprehensive assistive facilities for guests with disabilities. Spanning across a lush expanse of up to a hectare, our verdant grounds offer a tranquil retreat, complete with a dedicated children's entertainment area, ensuring an enriching experience for guests of all ages.
